Frequently Asked Questions

Do you support European brands?

Yes. We specialize in European brands (VAG, BMW/MINI, Mercedes-Benz, JLR, Bentley, Lamborghini) and also support American brands like Ford, GM/Chevrolet, Chrysler/Jeep/Dodge and Tesla for diagnostics and programming depending on model/year.

Do you use licensed software?

Yes. We support only licensed/authorized software and follow legal, documented procedures. Ownership/VIN verification may be required for security-related jobs.

Do you provide online automated checkouts?

No. Our services are handled offline with customer coordination. For workshops, we can help set up official OEM subscriptions with either a one-time assisted login or a 30-day access window.
